Introduction

Welcome to the Sri Lanka Stay & Explore user manual. This guide will help you navigate our platform and make the most of the features available for planning your Sri Lankan adventure.

Our website offers comprehensive travel services including:

  • Transportation booking throughout Sri Lanka
  • Information about attractions and experiences
  • Luxury accommodation options
  • Travel tips and recommendations

This manual provides step-by-step instructions for using each feature of our website.

Getting Started

Our website is designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate. Here's a quick overview of the main sections:

  • Home: The main landing page with an overview of our services
  • Transport: Book transportation services throughout Sri Lanka
  • Explore: Discover attractions and experiences in Sri Lanka
  • Contact: Get in touch with our team
  • Login/My Bookings: Access your account and view your bookings

The main navigation menu at the top of the page lets you quickly access these sections. On mobile devices, you can tap the menu icon in the top-right corner to access the navigation menu.

Booking Transport

Our transport booking system allows you to easily arrange transportation throughout Sri Lanka. Whether you need an airport transfer, city tour, or custom journey, we've got you covered.

Selecting Locations

To book transport, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the Transport section by clicking "Transport" in the navigation menu or the "Book Transport" button on the homepage.
  2. Fill out the booking form with your requirements:
    • Select service type (Airport Transfer, City Tour, or Custom Journey)
    • Choose your desired date and time
    • Indicate the number of passengers
  3. Enter pickup and destination locations:
    • You can type the location directly
    • Or click "Select on Map" to choose the location visually on a map

Note: When using the map selection, you can search for specific places, zoom in/out, and click on the map to select your exact location.

Fare Calculation

Once you've entered your pickup and destination locations, click the "Get Quote" button to calculate the fare. The fare is calculated based on:

  • The actual driving distance between locations (not straight-line distance)
  • The service type selected
  • The number of passengers
  • Any applicable time-based multipliers (rush hour, night, weekend)

The quote will show:

  • The distance between your locations
  • The vehicle type assigned
  • The estimated total fare
  • The required deposit amount (30% of the total)

Booking Process

After reviewing your quote, follow these steps to complete your booking:

  1. Click the "Book Now" button
  2. Fill in your contact information
  3. Review your booking details
  4. Pay the 30% deposit using your preferred payment method
  5. Receive a booking confirmation email with all details

Important: The full amount is payable on the day of travel. Your booking is only confirmed after the deposit is received.

Exploring Sri Lanka

Our website offers rich content to help you discover the beauty and diversity of Sri Lanka.

Discover Section

The "Discover Sri Lanka" section showcases various attractions and experiences across the island. Here's how to use it:

  1. Navigate to the Explore section by clicking "Explore" in the navigation menu or the "Explore Sri Lanka" button on the homepage.
  2. Browse through the featured items showcasing Sri Lanka's attractions.
  3. Use the filter buttons to narrow down content by category:
    • Scenery: Natural landscapes and viewpoints
    • Wildlife: National parks and animal encounters
    • Culture: Temples, historical sites, and local traditions
    • Food: Culinary experiences and local dishes
    • Beach: Coastal areas and beach activities
  4. Click on any item to view more details about that attraction.

Photo Gallery

Our photo gallery provides visual inspiration for your Sri Lankan adventure:

  1. Browse through the main featured image at the top of the gallery.
  2. Navigate using the thumbnails below to view different images.
  3. Use the category filters to view images by theme.
  4. Click on any image to view it in full size with detailed information.

Accommodations

We offer a selection of premium accommodations throughout Sri Lanka.

To explore our accommodation options:

  1. Scroll to the "Luxurious Accommodations" section on the homepage.
  2. Browse through the featured room types and properties.
  3. Click on any accommodation option to view more details, including:
    • Room features and amenities
    • Pricing information
    • Availability
    • Photos of the property

For booking accommodations, you can either:

  • Click the "Book Now" button on the accommodation details page
  • Contact our team directly through the Contact section
  • Ask our AI Assistant for help with booking

User Account

Registration

Creating an account allows you to manage your bookings and access additional features:

  1. Click "Login" in the navigation menu
  2. Select "Create Account" or "Register"
  3. Fill in the registration form with your:
    • Full name
    • Email address
    • Password
    • Contact information
  4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
  5. Once verified, you can log in to your account

Login

To access your account:

  1. Click "Login" in the navigation menu
  2. Enter your email address and password
  3. Click the "Login" button

If you forget your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check your email for password reset instructions
  4. Create a new password and log in

My Bookings

Once logged in, you can access your bookings:

  1. Click "My Bookings" in the navigation menu
  2. View a list of all your past and upcoming bookings
  3. Click on any booking to view detailed information
  4. From the booking details page, you can:
    • Download your booking confirmation
    • Modify your booking (subject to availability)
    • Cancel your booking (subject to cancellation policy)
    • Contact support regarding your booking

AI Travel Assistant

Our AI Travel Assistant is designed to provide instant help and information about Sri Lanka and our services.

To use the AI Assistant:

  1. Click the "Need Help?" button located in the bottom right corner of any page
  2. The AI Assistant chat window will open
  3. Type your question or request in the text box at the bottom of the chat window
  4. Press Enter or click the send button to submit your question
  5. The AI will respond with relevant information and assistance

The AI Assistant can help with:

  • Information about Sri Lankan attractions and experiences
  • Details about our transport and accommodation services
  • Booking assistance and recommendations
  • Travel tips and guidance
  • General questions about Sri Lanka

Note: The AI Assistant is designed to provide information and guidance. For complex bookings or specific inquiries, it may recommend contacting our team directly.

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues while using our website, here are some common solutions:

Map Loading Issues

  • Ensure your browser allows location services
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser

Booking Form Problems

  • Make sure all required fields are filled out
  • Check that your dates are valid (not in the past)
  • Ensure both pickup and destination locations are specified
  • If the form still doesn't submit, try using a different browser

Payment Issues

  • Verify your payment details are entered correctly
  • Ensure your card is not expired and has sufficient funds
  • Check if your bank is blocking the transaction
  • Try using a different payment method

Account Access Problems

  • Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" link
  • Check that you're using the correct email address
  • Ensure your account has been verified
  • Clear browser cookies and try again

Important: If you continue to experience issues, please contact our support team using the information in the Contact Us section.
