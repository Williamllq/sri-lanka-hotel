Introduction

Welcome to the Sri Lanka Stay & Explore website! This manual will guide you through the various features and help you make the most of your experience planning your Sri Lankan adventure.

Navigating the Website

The main navigation bar at the top of the page allows you to access different sections:

  • Home: Returns you to the main landing page.
  • Transport: Access information and booking for transport services.
  • Explore: Discover popular destinations and attractions in Sri Lanka through our gallery.
  • Contact: Find our contact information.
  • Login/My Bookings: Access your account or view your existing bookings (appears after login).

The Ranga Bandara logo also serves as a link to the Home page.

Booking Transport Services

Our website makes it easy to book reliable transport for your travels in Sri Lanka.

Finding Transport Information

In the "Transport Services" section (accessible via the "Transport" navigation link or by scrolling down on the homepage), you can find details about:

  • Vehicle Quality: Information about our well-maintained and comfortable vehicles.
  • Expert Drivers: Details about our professional, English-speaking local drivers.
  • Safety and Protection: Our commitment to your security.
  • Insider Knowledge: How our drivers can help you discover hidden gems.

Booking Your Journey

The booking form is located within the "Transport" section.

  1. Service Type: Choose from Airport Transfer, City Tour, or Custom Journey.
  2. Date and Time: Select your desired date and time for the service.
  3. Passengers: Indicate the number of people traveling.
  4. Pickup Location: Enter your pickup address or click " Select on Map" to choose a location from an interactive map. Search for locations or click directly on the map in the modal, then confirm.
  5. Destination: Enter your destination address or use the " Select on Map" feature.
  6. Special Requirements: Add any specific needs or requests (e.g., child seat, extra luggage).

Getting a Quote and Booking

  • Click "Get Quote" to see an estimated price based on your inputs. The quote will display the distance, vehicle type, estimated total, and the required 30% deposit. The route may also be displayed on a map.
  • If you are satisfied with the quote, click "Book Now" to proceed with the booking (this may require login/registration).

A 30% deposit is required at the time of booking. The full amount is payable on the day of travel.

Exploring Sri Lanka (Discover Section)

The "Discover Sri Lanka" section (accessible via the "Explore" navigation link or by scrolling) showcases the beauty and diversity of the island.

  • Filter Gallery: Use the filter buttons (All, Scenery, Wildlife, Culture, Food, Beach) to view images related to specific interests.
  • Featured Image: A large image is displayed with a title and description.
  • Thumbnails: Click on any thumbnail image below the featured image to view it in the main featured image area.

Luxurious Accommodations

Browse our selection of curated accommodations.

  • Each listing shows the room/suite type (e.g., Ocean View Suite, Tropical Garden Suite).
  • Key details like bed type, room size, and WiFi availability are provided.
  • Pricing is indicated (e.g., "From $250/night").
  • Currently, direct booking for accommodations through this section might require contacting us.

Contact Us & Give Feedback

Contact Information

The "Contact Us" section (accessible via the "Contact" navigation link or by scrolling) provides our:

  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • WhatsApp Number

Giving Feedback

We value your opinion! To share your experience:

  1. Click the " Give Feedback" button, usually found floating at the bottom left of the screen.
  2. A modal window will appear.
  3. Fill in your "Name" and "Country".
  4. Select your "Rating" using the stars (5 stars is best).
  5. Write your "Feedback" in the text area.
  6. Click " Submit Feedback".

Your Account (Login & My Bookings)

Login/Logout

  • Click the " Login" button in the main navigation.
  • If you have an account, enter your credentials. If not, you may find an option to register.
  • Once logged in, the button may change to show your username or a logout option. Clicking it again will allow you to logout.

My Bookings

  • After logging in, a " My Bookings" link will appear in the navigation.
  • Click this link to view your past and current transport bookings, check their status, and manage them if options are available.

AI Assistant (Need Help?)

Get instant help and information using our AI Travel Assistant.

  • Click the " Need Help?" button, usually floating at the bottom right of the screen.
  • A chat window will open.
  • Type your questions about Sri Lanka, our services, or your travel plans in the input field.
  • Press Enter or click the send button () to get a response.
  • You can minimize or close the AI assistant window using the controls in its header.
